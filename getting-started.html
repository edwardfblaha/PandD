FAQ

Common Questions

Who can rent from P&D?

We rent exclusively to FedEx Ground and FedEx Home Delivery contractors. Our vehicles, service plans and support are all built around FedEx routes.

What kind of vehicles can I rent?

Our fleet is primarily shelved step vans — the P 700, P 900 and P 1000 — purpose-built for the movement, pickup and delivery of packages.

Is there a mileage limit?

No. P&D rentals include unlimited mileage so you can run the routes your business needs.

What about maintenance?

We provide onsite maintenance, including convenient mobile mechanical and tire service, to keep your vehicle on the road.

How long can I keep a vehicle?

Your rental renews automatically for as long as you have the vehicle—there’s no need to re-book each period.

How do I get help?

We’re available 24/7. Call 1-844-PDTRUCK or email info@pdtruckrental.com.
