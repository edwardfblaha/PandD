About P&D Truck Rental

A fleet built from the ground up to serve the parcel-delivery market.

Who We Are

Built for Parcel Delivery

P&D Truck Rental specializes in parcel delivery. All of our vehicles — primarily shelved step vans — are designed for the movement, pickup and delivery of packages. The step van is by far the most efficient design for high-volume parcel delivery, greatly outperforming cargo vans and box trucks.

Our affiliate network plays a mission-critical role in the parcel-delivery ecosystem, supplying vehicles to major logistics networks operated by companies such as Amazon, DHL, FedEx, UPS and USPS.
